About Us

The Placement Cell of IIIT Vadodara provides a platform to students and recruiters to come together and explore mutual interest and alliance. The cell closely works with students for their internship and job opportunities and at the same time with recruiters to provide best industry ready students.




Director's Message

I, on behalf of IIIT Vadodara, welcome your organisation to the institute for recruiting the third undergraduate batch of students. I also welcome you to recruit pioneer batch postgraduate students. IIIT Vadodara enters into sixth year of existence. In these infancy years our students have contributed immensely.
Our undergraduate academic programs; B Tech in Computer Science Eng and Information Technology have a progressive curriculum with balance of theory and lab courses, project work along with industry experience through internship and industry project. The institute has a vibrant academic environment. The institute has all having 100% ...faculty members with PhD degree. Many of our students have succeeded in international and national events like Gooogle Summer Code (GSoC), Smart India Hackathon name a few. I am sure, our students will contribute in taking your company to greater heights.
I also request all industries to contribute to our academic curriculum in terms of suggestions, offering industry courses in making out graduates industry ready. I also invite you to participate in joint research initiative. I once again welcome you to our institute.
